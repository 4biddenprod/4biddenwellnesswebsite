Shipping Policy

Order Processing and Production

All our items are print-on-demand, which means each product is uniquely made after your order is placed. Order processing typically takes 3-7 business days. After processing, your order is shipped out.

Shipping Rates and Delivery Estimates

Economy Delivery

  • Cost: USD 3.99 – 7.39 (depending on the item)
  • Coverage: United States only
  • Delivery Time:
    • Local (United States): 4–8 business days
    • International: Not available

Standard Delivery

  • Cost: USD 4.75 – 9.99 (depending on the item)
  • Coverage: Worldwide
  • Delivery Time:
    • Local (United States): 2–5 business days
    • International: 10–30 business days

Express Delivery

  • Cost: USD 7.99 (available on select items only)
  • Coverage: United States only
  • Delivery Time:
    • Local (United States): 2–3 business days
    • International: Not available

Customs, Duties, and Taxes

International orders may incur customs and tax fees that are not included in our prices. These fees are assessed by your local customs office and are your responsibility. Please check with your country’s customs office to determine if there will be additional costs.

Tracking Your Order

You will receive a tracking link via email once your order ships out. If you have any questions about your tracking or shipment, please contact us at Contact@4biddenprod.com.

Missing Orders

If your order hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe:

  • Check your shipping confirmation email for errors in the delivery address.
  • Inquire with your local post office.
  • Check with your neighbors to see if the courier left your package with them.

If everything was correct and you still haven't received your package, please contact us at Contact@4biddenprod.com with your order number.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please email us within a week at Contact@4biddenprod.com with photos of the items, your order number, and any other relevant information. We will work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
